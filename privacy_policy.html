PromptLens — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

PromptLens is a Chrome extension that analyzes images using the OpenRouter AI API and returns a structured JSON prompt. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed and how.

Data We Collect

PromptLens does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the extension developer. Specifically:

  • Your OpenRouter API key is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.sync and is never sent to our servers.
  • Images you analyze are sent directly from your browser to the OpenRouter API. We do not intercept or store them.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking scripts, or any form of telemetry.

Third-Party Services

When you analyze an image, it is sent to OpenRouter for processing. OpenRouter's own privacy policy applies to that data. Please review it at openrouter.ai/privacy.

Permissions Used

  • contextMenus: To add the right-click menu item on images.
  • activeTab / scripting: To copy the result to your clipboard and show a notification on the current page.
  • storage: To save your API key locally in the browser.
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>): To download and encode any image you right-click on, regardless of the website.
